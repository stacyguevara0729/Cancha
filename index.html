Our
Story
Where it began

A setback became a movement.

Cancha was born from pain — and from the stubborn belief that pain doesn't have to be the end. When Stacy Guevara was told to stop playing the sport she loved, she turned her grief into action.

She discovered that while she was mourning losing access to soccer, thousands of children in Central America had never had access at all — no cleats, no balls, no field to call their own.

Cancha exists to close that gap. We receive donations and send them directly to youth soccer programs across Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Panama, and Belize — while also supporting athletes here who are fighting to stay in the game.

What we do

Two communities. One mission.

We connect athletes who have lost their place in sport with kids who never had one to begin with — and we turn that connection into real, lasting change.

⚽

Gear for Central America

We collect and fund soccer equipment — cleats, jerseys, balls, shin guards — and send them directly to youth programs across all 7 countries of Central America through verified partner organizations.

🫂

Athlete recovery support

Through our Back in the Game app and community, we support athletes dealing with injuries, self-doubt, and setbacks — pairing them with mentors who have been through it and come out the other side.

🌱

Long-term community building

Cancha isn't a one-time drive. We're building an organization that grows year over year — expanding to new communities, new chapters, and new partnerships across Central America and beyond.

About Cancha

Built by a student. For everyone.

Cancha is a youth-led nonprofit organization founded on Long Island, New York by a student athlete who was told her soccer career was over — and decided to do something bigger with that pain.

The name Cancha comes from the Spanish word for soccer field. Because that's what we're really giving — not just equipment, but access. Access to a field, to a team, to the feeling of belonging that sport gives every kid who gets to play.

We are not a one-time fundraiser. Cancha is an organization built to last — growing its chapter network, deepening its partnerships in Central America, and expanding its reach year after year.

SG
Stacy Guevara Founder & Executive Director, Cancha

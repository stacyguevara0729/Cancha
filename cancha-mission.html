Why Cancha exists.

Every child deserves access to sport. Every athlete deserves a second chance. This is how we make both happen — and why it matters more than ever.

The problem

Millions of kids can't afford to play.

Across Central America, soccer is more than a sport. It's culture, community, and for many children, the only safe place they have. But access to equipment — even a pair of cleats — is out of reach for millions of families living in poverty.

60%
of children in Central America live below the poverty line, making basic sports equipment completely unaffordable for most families.
7
countries across Central America where youth soccer programs are desperately underfunded and undersupplied.
  • 1
    Children in rural communities often play barefoot on concrete or dirt lots with no proper ball, no cleats, and no protective equipment.
  • 2
    Youth soccer programs in Guatemala, Honduras, and Nicaragua frequently shut down not because of lack of passion — but lack of gear.
  • 3
    Without access to organized sport, children in at-risk communities lose one of the most powerful tools for staying safe, healthy, and hopeful.
  • 4
    A single pair of cleats can cost more than a week's wages for a family — meaning the beautiful game stays out of reach for the kids who love it most.
Our solution

What Cancha does about it.

Cancha is a youth-led nonprofit that collects and funds soccer gear, distributes it through verified partner organizations on the ground, and builds a community of athlete storytellers who fund every drive.

01

We receive donations

Through our GoFundMe campaign and Cancha website, we collect funds from donors who believe every kid deserves a field to play on. Every dollar is tracked and reported.

02

We purchase gear

We work with partner organizations like AFJA and GlobalGiving to identify exactly what each community needs — cleats, jerseys, balls, shin guards — and purchase it directly.

03

We ship & distribute

Gear is sent through verified local organizations in each country who distribute it directly to youth soccer programs, schools, and community leagues where kids are waiting.

04

We share their stories

Through the Back in the Game app, we share real updates from the children who received gear — so every donor can see the face behind their donation and the difference it made.

Transparency

Exactly how your donation helps.

We believe donors deserve to know exactly where their money goes. Here is the complete journey of every dollar donated to Cancha.

1

Your donation arrives

Donations come in through our GoFundMe campaign. 100% of funds collected go directly toward purchasing soccer gear — no overhead, no administrative fees taken from donations. Cancha is a student-led organization operating on zero salary.

2

We identify the need

Through our partner organizations already on the ground in Central America, we identify which communities have the most urgent need — which programs are running out of gear, which kids are playing barefoot, which leagues are about to shut down.

3

Gear is purchased & shipped

We purchase cleats, jerseys, shin guards, soccer balls, and cones — the essentials that make organized soccer possible. Gear is sourced locally where possible to support local economies and reduce shipping costs.

4

Kids receive it directly

Partner organizations distribute gear directly to youth at their academies and community programs. We verify every delivery and share photos and updates so donors can see exactly who their generosity reached.

5

Stories come back to the community

The children who receive gear share their stories through the Back in the Game app — inspiring athletes here who are going through their own setbacks, and closing the loop between two communities that need each other.
